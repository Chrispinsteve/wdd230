Site Plan for I-Business Chamber of Commerce

I-Business Chamber

I Business Chamber of Commerce is the name of the Site Plan designed to support the business community in Bordeaux.

Purpose of the Site

The site aims to support local businesses and promote the I Business community. The primary goals are as follows:

  • Provide resources and services to member businesses.
  • Offer information on Chamber events and networking opportunities.

Scenarios

Here are some questions that visitors might ask to guide the site's content:

  • What business-to-business events are hosted this month?
  • Where can I find contact information for Chamber board members?

Click here to get the wireframe for large (page 1) and small (page 2) screens.

Color Scheme

The selected colors for this site are:

  • Gray: For titles and important elements.
  • White and gray: For the main background.
  • Red: For navigation and the footer.

Typography

The selected fonts for this site are:

  • Roboto: For headings and subheadings.
  • Open Sans: For body text.
