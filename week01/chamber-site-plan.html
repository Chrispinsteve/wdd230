Site Plan for I-Business Chamber of Commerce

I-Business Chamber

I Business Chamber of Commerce

This name was chosen to represent the city of Bordeaux, which has a dynamic business community and a rich history. This website will serve as a central point for local businesses and the city's economic initiatives.

Purpose of the Site

The purpose of the site is to provide services to local businesses, act as an economic catalyst, and promote the I Business community. The primary goals of the site are as follows:

  • Provide resources and services to member businesses to help them grow.
  • Provide information on Chamber of Commerce events and networking activities.

Scenarios

Here are some questions that visitors to the site might ask, which will guide the content of the site:

  • What events are organized by the Chamber of Commerce this month to promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Chamber's board members?
  • How does Bordeaux's population growth impact the local economy?

Color Scheme

The color scheme selected for this site includes the following colors:

  • Gray: Used for titles and important elements.
  • White and gray: Used for the main background of the site.
  • Red: Used for nav a and in the footer of the site.

These colors reflect the professionalism and sobriety of the I Business Chamber of Commerce.

Typography

The typography selected for this site includes the following fonts:

  • Roboto: Used for headings and subheadings.
  • Open Sans: Used for the body text.

These fonts are chosen for their readability and modernity.
